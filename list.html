Unordered List

This is an unordered list. To create:

  1. type ul with < >
  2. inside the ul tag is the li tag. To create, type li with < > in between the list item. You must use this with every list item.

E.X. 1

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 cups sugar
  • 3/4 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons baking soda
  • 1 teaspoon salt
